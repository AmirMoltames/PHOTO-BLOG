HEY, I’M THE PHOTOGRAPHER

But I don't really care about photography. It's what photography does that captivates me.
It has written a big part of my story. In the last ten years photography has taken me all over the world. It has brought people and places into my life that have changed me.

Photography made me fall in love with the country I call home now. I'm an Italian who grew up in Germany and lives by the english seaside.

Photography makes me seek beauty, create joy and find magic in the mundane. When I create, I belong. I am home. This purpose is what guides my work. All I want is to create a moment of human connection.
